 Title: RIDGID R84015 X2 Planetary Gear Replacement Guide
 URL: https://www.ifixit.com/Guide/RIDGID+R84015+X2+Planetary+Gear+Replacement+Guide/2675

 Required Tools:
- phillips 2 screwdriver (http://www.ifixit.com/Tools/Phillips-2-Screwdriver/IF145-032)
- t20 torx screwdriver (https://www.ifixit.com/Store/Tools/54-Bit-Driver-Kit/IF145-299)

 Steps:

Step 0:
- using a torx t20 screwdriver , remove the four screws attaching the back plate .

Step 1:
- remove the five screws attaching the drill handle .

Step 2:
- remove the 4 screws attaching the front of the casing .

Step 3:
- unscrew the four screws at the rear of the plastic gear chamber .

Step 4:
- remove the motor from the drill .
- note : handle the gear chamber with caution as the gears are now loose .

Step 5:
- remove the four screws that attach the plastic gear chamber .
- remove the plastic gear chamber .

Step 6:
- remove each component from the gear assembly .
